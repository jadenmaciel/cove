## Architecture

```
Keyboard Shortcut (Ctrl+Tab)
        |
        v
KeyboardShortcutSettings.Action.nextWorkspace
        |
        v
TabManager.nextWorkspace() / prevWorkspace()
        |
        v
TabManager.selectedTabId = nextWorkspaceId
        |
        v
Sidebar re-renders (observation on selectedTabId)
```

Sidebar click:
```
Sidebar Click on Workspace Row
        |
        v
onTapGesture / Button action
        |
        v
tabManager.selectedTabId = clickedWorkspace.id
```
